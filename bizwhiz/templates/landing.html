Features
Everything you need to fill your pipeline
From finding leads to closing deals, BizWhiz handles the whole workflow.

Local Business Search

Enter any ZIP code and business type. BizWhiz finds every matching business within your chosen radius using OpenStreetMap data.

Automatic Email Scraping

BizWhiz visits each business website and extracts real contact emails automatically — no copy-pasting required.

Built-in CRM Pipeline

Track every lead through New, Contacted, Proposal Sent, Negotiating, and Closed stages with a visual pipeline board.

Email Outreach

Send personalized emails directly from BizWhiz using your own SMTP. Save templates and track what you've sent.

CSV Import & Export

Import your existing lead lists or export everything to CSV for use in other tools. Full flexibility with your data.

Dashboard Analytics

See your outreach rate, win rate, pipeline breakdown, and recent activity at a glance from your dashboard.
